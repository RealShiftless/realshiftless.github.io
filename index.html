About me

I am Aaron Moulding, a 21 year old Audio Engineering student at the Herman Brood Academie in the Netherlands. I'm very passionate about electronic music and try to expand my self creatively where ever I can. Currently I'm working to get my spot in the electronic music industry as a technician, and as an artist.

I've been producing music for about 6 years now, it started as a hobby and a way to vent, to a passion that I want to commit my life to. Most of my skills have come from self teaching and YouTube, which makes me confident in saying that I have developed my own sound, which I can refine over time. I also find graphics very important to convey emotion in your music, this is why I am also slowly learning graphic design to better my understanding of design.

I've also programmed for about half my life as a hobby, generally in the area of games and game engines, creative programming, and as you can see also websites. Because of this I have a good sense of problem solving.

What am I doing currently?

I'm currently preparing to play my tracks live, the end goal of this is to for example stream and create content about electronic music. To achieve this I have been creating an ableton template with the needed midi mappings and tracks with macros for my Push 2, in which I can use my push, BCR2000 and max for live as a digital modular synths of sorts.

I'm also working on finalising tracks for my next album, creating a more professional sound than before by also mastering my tracks. I've recently passed for my audio engineering exam making me more confident than before that I can make a well mixed album, which will perhaps be released on a seperate artist project.
